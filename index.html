Welcome to Absolute Blast Corp. Sandblasting!

Sandblasting is probably the dirtiest and most dangerous occupation in the renovation; restoration trades. The dangers of the work and the danger of damaging sensitive projects is not to be taken lightly. ABSOLUTE BLAST CORP. has trained its team in the art of cleaning and all the safety aspects needed in mobile sandblasting and is proud of the young men who have achieved the proficiency that is required in the sandblasting field. This is especially important when our mobile blasting takes us into heritage restoration work where delicate substrates as in soft brick, carved stone and facade patina have to be considered.

From heavy equipment cleaning using hard grit, delicate monument restoration to graffiti removal using widely varied specialized methods and using soda, soft media, walnut, dry ice, wet blasting plus power washing and with a painting crew to deal with what ever you need ABSOLUTE BLAST CO. is Ontario's trusted choice in sandblasting.

Being part of the Heritage Facade Restoration Guild has put our team in a standard of their own. There are constant improvements in training and equipment as the heritage restoration field sees new and better methods being introduced. The Rotec Systems (AKA as the JOS System) are now coming to the fore in the area as architects and heritage groups see the wisdom of having qualified people clean their projects. Plus the expertise in handling interior blasting with temporary blast houses and negative air pressure systems etc. is just part of the ABSOLUTE BLAST CORP. offerings to our customers.

Serving Southern and Central Ontario, including Toronto, North Bay, Sudbury, Huntsville, Hamilton, Burlington, Kitchener, Waterloo, London, Mississauga, and other neighboring areas. We are available throughout Ontario and beyond, depending on the project.
